🌍 Eco-Friendly Living

Small Steps, Big Impact

💡 Save Electricity

Why Save Electricity?

Saving electricity helps reduce pollution and conserves natural resources. By using energy wisely, we can protect our environment and save money.

What Can We Do?

  • Switch off lights and fans when leaving a room.
  • Use LED bulbs instead of regular bulbs.
  • Unplug chargers when not in use.

Benefits

  • Lower electricity bills
  • Less pollution
  • Conservation of resources

📄 Use Both Sides of Paper

Why Is It Important?

Paper is made from trees. Using both sides of paper reduces waste and helps protect forests.

What Can We Do?

  • Write on both sides of notebook pages.
  • Reuse old sheets for rough work.
  • Avoid unnecessary printing.

Benefits

  • Saves trees
  • Reduces waste
  • Promotes responsible habits

🌱 Conclusion

Protecting the environment starts with simple daily actions. By saving electricity and using both sides of paper, we can help build a cleaner, greener future for everyone.
